The Business Model Canvas

Key Partners

Key business partners include those assisting in the development and creation of the project who include all members of the Code Toad project. It also includes GitHub which allows us to store and host the code for our project. A key partner also includes our instructor. Partners are involved in creation and development of the idea into a working web application.

Key Activities

This project will require organizing the features of the neighborhood watch application into user stories. It will require iterative development of these user stories in order to create the neighborhood watch application.

Value Proposition

This will provide a solution to enable neighbors to be aware of safety and security concerns in their neighborhood. It will be a useful project for the course CSIS 2450 to allow my group to get a grade and pass this course.

Customer Relationship

Our customers expect us to deliver a high quality application that enables neighborhoods to communicate critical information at the time of emergency. Currently we have not established relationships with those who will use the product. However those on the team will be willing to use the product in their neighborhoods.

Customer Segments

Those customers that we are creating value include citizens throughout the Salt Lake Valley that are interested in the safety and security of their neighborhood. They are are most important customers.
